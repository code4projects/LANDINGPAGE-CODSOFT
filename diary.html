    SUHAIB AHMAD

As the sun began to set, casting a warm golden glow over the horizon,
the journey of young Suhaib Ahmad came to an end. With a heart full of excitement and anticipation, he stepped onto the foreign land that beckoned him with its mysteries and wonders. The air was thick with a medley of unfamiliar scents, carrying the promise of new experiences.
As he took in his surroundings, his eyes widened with awe at the vibrant colors and bustling streets that stretched out before him. With each step, Suhaib felt a surge of determination, ready to embrace the challenges that awaited him and embark on a journey of self-discovery.
This landing marked the beginning of a transformative adventure, where Suhaib would weave his own tale of courage, resilience, and growth.
